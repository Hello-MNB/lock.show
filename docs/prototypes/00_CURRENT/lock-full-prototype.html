End-to-end

The complete flow

One story, in order — from an empty Act to a paying booking manager. Every step is clickable: open the screen, or follow the cross-entity handoff to see the same moment from the other side.

1
Artist

Artist onboards & opens the Radar

A DJ signs up as a Person, creates their first Act, and lands on the Radar — six planets, the platform ring, the milestone journey. Evidence is per-Act and starts empty.

2
Artist

Adds evidence — AI extracts claims

The artist pastes a link / uploads a file. Per-evidence Anthropic extraction returns method-labeled ✦found claims (verified / supporting / self-reported). Nothing publishes as fact until confirmed.

3
Source-Confirmer

A source confirms one claim

The producer who ran the show gets a bounded magic link — no account. They read ONE claim and reply Yes / Partly / No. A Yes stamps the canon label PRODUCER-CONFIRMED onto that single statement.

4
Artist

Artist publishes the Passport

With confirmed evidence in place, the artist reviews the Artist view (gaps shown) and publishes a snapshot. The Buyer view exposes verified strengths only.

5
Buyer

Buyer opens the Passport — no login

A booking manager receives the share link and opens the public Passport with no account. 30-second scan: hero, two proofs, source-peek on hover. Draw = bands + binaries + method labels.

6
Buyer

Buyer requests availability — the Gate

The buyer sends a short availability request (name · event · date). This is THE Gate action — it lands in the artist's Requests inbox.

7
Representation

Representation manages the roster

An artist-side office holds the Act on its roster via a consented, revocable ArtistAccess grant — never ownership. Each row is a guided next-action card; the role-adapted lens shifts the register.

8
Production

Production evaluates before booking

A production office builds an event and, per open slot, pulls the artist's method-labeled Passport context — fit, reliability, source context, availability — to decide before risking its name.

9
Admin

Admin sees the signal

The operator cockpit shows the business at a glance — a booking manager reacting to a real Passport AND paying is the Gate. Counts and SLA are ops-side; the firewall still forbids any user-facing score.

🔒 Reaction insight returning to the artist is method-safe text only — never a count, %, or score. The Gate = one booking manager reacts to a real Passport AND one pays.
