# **1. GIGPROOF IN ONE SENTENCE**

GIGPROOF is a consent-based pre-booking intelligence and trust infrastructure for the Israeli live-music industry. It transforms fragmented live-performance evidence into bounded, method-labelled intelligence that helps booking professionals evaluate an unfamiliar artist before they risk a recommendation, shortlist or booking decision.

  

GIGPROOF is not an EPK template, generic artist profile, public ranking, booking guarantee, marketplace, booking CRM or post-booking operations system.

  

PRE-BOOKING INTELLIGENCE VALUE LOCK

The product loop is: consented evidence → source semantics and limitations → artist review/correction → private Artist Radar intelligence and next action → artist-approved Bookability Passport → explicit professional action or missing-proof signal → confirmation/outcome learning → evidence refresh. Proof is an input and public output; decision intelligence and learning are the system value.

  

# **2. CURRENT PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE**

## **Artist Radar**

The artist-private recurring-value product. It scans and structures the artist's lawful professional footprint, assets, positioning, bookable offer, evidence, commercial facts, technical readiness, opportunity-specific materials and post-performance learning. It identifies gaps and provides non-shaming next-best actions.

  

## **Mirror**

A private diagnostic state/view inside Artist Radar. It is not a substitute product and must not narrow Radar into evidence maintenance only.

  

## **Bookability Passport**

The buyer-facing curated decision output produced from artist-approved supported strengths. It is one output of the Radar, not the whole product. Publication requires at least two supported Proof Units, including at least one live-demand or commercial-evidence Proof Unit such as Attributed Draw, ticket activity, repeat booking, a supported fee/guarantee band or counterparty-confirmed live activity. Identity, biography, media and streaming/social context alone never qualify a Passport for publication.

  

## **Agency Roster Radar**

A separate organization-scoped capability for roster readiness, evidence freshness and consented inbound-demand triage across artists covered by active ArtistAccess. "Roster Intelligence" may be used as its UI descriptor, but Agency Roster Radar is the canonical capability name.

  

## **Similar-Artist / Cohort Comparison**

PROPOSED — PENDING MARIA APPROVAL. This private Radar capability is not approved build scope. If approved later, AI may propose candidates while deterministic rules validate eligibility. No public ranking, score, percentile, prediction or guarantee.

# **\# 2A. CATEGORY LOCK — WHAT GIGPROOF IS / IS NOT (ANTI-DRIFT — READ FIRST)**

Why this exists: every general AI model, working without this Canon in context, rebuilds this product as a score, ranking, or marketplace by default — because that is what the category looks like from the outside. This has already happened twice (a GPT "6-Axis" line and a Gemini line, both quarantined). Any output — spec, screen, prototype, doc — that contains a WE-ARE-NOT item is DRIFT and must be rejected on sight, regardless of how well written or well disclaimered it is. A disclaimer saying "this is not a score" under something that behaves like a score does not make it compliant.

WE ARE:

  

A consent-based, pre-booking intelligence and trust infrastructure for the Israeli live-music industry.

A producer of bounded, method-labelled evidence: bands + binaries, each with a method label, context, and reviewed date.

Two products from one evidence base — the private Artist Radar (console + intelligence) and the public Bookability Passport (curated output).

Upstream of the booking decision: we inform a professional's judgement; we never transact, hold, or guarantee a booking.

Zero-party: the artist discloses their own data by consent.

  

WE ARE NOT (each line is a hard firewall breach if it appears on any surface):

  

NOT a score / percentile / rank / rating / grade / gauge / probability / prediction / "bookability %".

NOT a per-dimension numeric fill (0–100, %, or equivalent) even in a private coaching view.

NOT a cohort / "you vs segment vs target" / artist-vs-artist comparison on any surface. (Private cohort comparison is PROPOSED-only, disabled, pending Maria.)

NOT a marketplace, discovery feed, directory, or availability/flash-bidding auction.

NOT a two-way ratings or review system. The producer/מפיק is a confirmer, never a rated party.

NOT an escrow, payment gateway, auto-invoicing, booking commission, or payment-routing system.

NOT a scraper: no scraping, no unofficial public-metadata reads, no cron profile-tracking, no graph-mapping. Metadata enters only via official API, consented OAuth, or artist-provided artifact — or artist-confirmed discovery, where the system may SURFACE a web-found candidate for the artist to confirm or reject, but NEVER publishes web-sourced data as fact without the artist's explicit confirmation and method-label. Automated discovery runs per-artist, opt-in, only after consent, and requires counsel sign-off on Amendment 13 before build. Cold-crawling or holding a directory of non-onboarded people remains prohibited.

NOT an EPK, generic artist profile, booking CRM, or post-booking operations system.

NOT a booking guarantee. Payment buys processing, workflow, and entitlement — never credibility or a stronger evidence label.

NOT a global platform yet: beachhead = Israeli scene, one pilot artist (SHIGAON), three bookers (Yossi, Yorav, Oshri).

  

THE THREE CORE OBJECTS (locked definitions):

  

Artist Radar — the artist-private, recurring-value console. The artist sees their whole professional self organized by dimension (identity, attributed draw, community, live/performance, readiness, fee-context, opportunity fit, momentum), and per dimension: sees the evidence state, sees the gap, adds/refreshes evidence, and takes the next-best action. The "radar" is an interaction and navigation surface, not a chart. It renders dimension state ONLY as named evidence states or linear evidence tracks — NEVER as a numeric-fill polygon and NEVER with a cohort overlay. "Mirror" is one diagnostic view inside Radar, not a separate product.

Bookability Passport — the buyer-facing curated output, rendered per viewing persona, containing artist-approved supported strengths only, read-only to external viewers. Publish requires ≥2 supported Proof Units incl. ≥1 live-demand/commercial unit.

Widget (Proof Unit module) — every Radar dimension and every Passport block is ONE self-contained, reusable module with a single data contract. The SAME widget renders two faces: inside the Radar it is editable, shows gaps + next-action; on the Passport it is read-only, method-labelled, strengths-only. Screens are compositions of widgets, not bespoke pages. One widget = one Claim-cluster = one DB contract.
