# GIGPROOF — Glossary & Terminology (SSOT)
### The words we use, why, and the Hebrew that sounds native to the IL scene

**By:** R16 · **Date:** 24 Jun 2026 · **Rule:** every artifact, message, website, and pitch uses these terms. If a term changes, change it HERE first (update in place), then propagate. This prevents the same role being called three different things across documents.

---

## 1 · THE CORE ROLES (the distinction that matters most)
The English word "booker" lumps together roles that the Israeli scene keeps separate — and they carry *different risk*, which is central to our positioning. Pick the right one every time.

| Concept (EN) | ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use (HE) | When to use | Risk they carry |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Booking agent / manager / agency | "בוקרים" (Anglicism, reads off) | **אמרגן / אמרגנים** · or **מנהל בוקינג / אנשי בוקינג** | The person/agency who books & represents artists, recommends them, risks their **reputation** on the pitch. **This is our primary beachhead (Yossi, Yorav).** | Reputation, time, pitch success |
| Event producer / promoter / venue | — | **מפיק / מפיקים** | The person running the event who carries the **direct financial risk** (rent, security, production). Demand-side underwriter. | Money — direct cash loss on a dead night |
| Artist manager | — | **מנהל אמן / מנהל אמנים** | Manages an artist's career (may pay to make the artist sellable). | Career outcome of their artist |
| Venue (the place) | — | **מועדון** (club) · **אולם** (hall) · **לוקיישן** | The physical place. | (the asset, not a person) |

**Why this matters commercially:** our pitch says "protect your name with the מפיקים you sell to" — that sentence only works if we correctly separate the **אמרגן** (sells the artist, risks their name) from the **מפיק** (buys the night, risks the money). Using "בוקרים" for both erases the exact distinction our positioning sells on.

**Default for Yossi-type pitches:** **אמרגנים** or **אנשי בוקינג**. **Default for the venue/risk side:** **מפיקים**. When you mean both: **"אמרגנים ומפיקים."**

## 6 · PROHIBITED WORDS (the firewall, in language)
Never write these in any artifact, HE or EN:
- ❌ "מבטיח הזמנות" / "guarantees bookings" / "ensures you'll fill the room"
- ❌ any prediction "%" shown to a user ("83% bookable")
- ❌ "venue-confirmed" / "independent proof" (we verify artist-supplied data; say **אימות סטנדרטי**)
- ❌ "בוקרים" (use אמרגנים / מפיקים per role)
- ❌ public "score" / "ציון" / "דירוג" / "percentile" shown to artist or buyer

## 8 · DEAL & MONEY TERMS (real scene-slang — adopt)

## 13 · TICKETING PLATFORMS & ATTRIBUTION MAP (field-verified 24 Jun 2026)
Which systems exist and whether they expose per-artist attribution. Built from real operator interviews — keep updating.

| Platform / method | Per-artist attribution? | Source (who told us) |
|---|---|---|
| **Selector** | Producer tracks via mandatory "where from?" checkout Q; artist can't see it; producer CAN export per-link | Shai Perlman (DJ+producer) |
| **Go-out** | Has the capability ("I have a tool for it") but many don't use it | Yehuda Narkis (TERRA producer) |
| **Eventer** | (named earlier; commission-based; assume per-link possible — VERIFY) | prior research |
| **קופות תל אביב** | "הכל חשוף" — fully transparent; artist sees their UTM sales | Yossi Jamal |
| **PayBox (cash)** | NONE — no ticketing system, cash collection | Tal Lippo / הכיתה הטיפולית |
| **Gagarin site** | Has UTM but operator doesn't use it | via Shai Perlman |

**Takeaway:** attribution transparency is INCONSISTENT across the market — some fully open (קופות ת"א), some producer-only (Selector), some none (PayBox cash). This inconsistency is *why the artist is usually blind to his own draw* — and a logged "change-the-market" opportunity (NOT to build now).
